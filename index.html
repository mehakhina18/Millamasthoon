Our Story

Where Tradition Meets Taste

✦

Nestled in the heart of Metroville, Karachi, Milmasthon Restaurant is a celebrated destination for those who crave the bold, soul-warming flavours of Afghani and Pakistani cuisine.

From slow-cooked Dumpukht and fragrant Afghani Pulao to sizzling BBQ platters and rich Karahi — every dish is a testament to culinary heritage, prepared with finest spices and time-honoured techniques.

Whether you're gathering with family, celebrating with friends, or seeking a comforting meal, Milmasthon offers an experience that goes beyond dining — it's a warm Pashtoon welcome on every plate.
